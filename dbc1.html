Codename: The Human Taurch

Alias: Sarah Taurchini

Early Life

Sarah was born in New Haven, Connnecticut in the wee hours of Holy Thursday in the year of 1986. She was a precocious thing at a young age and enjoyed school and reading a great deal. At age 3, she asked for her own laptop for Christmas. Her parents thought she was kidding.

School Days

    Taurchini attended the following schools

The Aftermath

Upon realizing during her senior year at Georgetown that anyone she knew who had a job offer got it because of nepotism, Sarah took the first job offered to her for after graduation: a role in a professional theatre production in Washington, DC.
